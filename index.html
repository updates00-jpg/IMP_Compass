Radio Procedures
Channel Usage
Stations scan Channel 1 simultaneously on their own user group channel. To place a call on Ch.1 — switch to Ch.1. After receiving a call on Ch.1 you can respond from your user channel within 4 seconds, or switch to Ch.1 to respond later. Language: English.
Daily Radio Check
JOC initiates each working day between 09:00–10:00. All control stations must respond, plus all other stations available at that time.

Convoy Rules — Quick Reference
CONVOY DEFINITION
≥ 7 vehicles = convoy. LOG MOV Officer (Cell) controls all convoys of 7+ vehicles, border crossings, tactical moves, oversized vehicles, ammo/explosives.
MOVEMENT REQUEST DEADLINES
Within AOO: 36 hours in advance to LOG MOV Officer.
Cross-border: 3 working days in advance.
Emergency: by 1800hrs day before minimum.
CONVOY SIZE LIMITS
Max 15 vehicles per packet. Convoys ≥16 vehicles: break into packets ≤15, release interval 15 min between packets. Multiple convoys same SP: 60 min between convoys.
CCN ON VEHICLES
CCN must be displayed on both sides of leading vehicle AND front and back of trailing vehicle. IMP verifies during escort and on-camp checks.
DELAY ≥ 30 MIN
Convoy Commander MUST immediately notify LOG MOV Officer (Cell) to obtain amended start time. LOG MOV notifies border control elements.
VEHICLE BREAKDOWN IN CONVOY
Column does NOT halt for single breakdown. Broken down vehicle: yellow flag + warning triangle + flasher lights. Driver stays with vehicle. Same-unit vehicles attempt recovery first. Recovery at rear of convoy.
OVERSIZED VEHICLES
Width >2.60m / Length >16.50m / Height >4.00m / Weight >MLC 40 = oversized. Requires LOG MOV approval regardless of convoy size.
IMP ESCORT TRIGGERS
IMP escort required when specified in Movement Request. Always required for: ammo/explosives convoys, VIP moves, fuel trucks, media personnel in coord with PAO. Request via FPM Office in advance (emergencies excepted).
TECHNICAL HALTS
Technical Halt (TH) every ~2 hours. Rest Halt (RH) after ~8 hours strongly recommended.
Movement Request — Required Fields
(1) Date of Movement
(2) Unit moving (e.g. MNBN/A-COY)
(3) Reason: Tactical / Operational / Routine
(4) Number of Vehicles
(5) Start Point (SP) — name of origin
(6) Estimated Time of Departure (ETD)
(7) Convoy Route — route code names
(8) Border Crossing Points (BXPs) if applicable
(9) ETA at BXPs
(10) Final Destination
(11) ETA at Final Destination
(12) Return Details (if same-day)
(13) Oversized Vehicles — W/H/L/Weight
(14) Remarks: MP escort needed? Croatian police? TCC responsible for co-ord?
(15) If >15 vehicles: vehicles per packet
