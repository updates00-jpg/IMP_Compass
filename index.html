Radio Procedures
Channel Usage
Stations scan Channel 1 simultaneously on their own user group channel. To place a call on Ch.1 — switch to Ch.1. After receiving a call on Ch.1 you can respond from your user channel within 4 seconds, or switch to Ch.1 to respond later. Language: English.
Daily Radio Check
JOC initiates each working day between 09:00–10:00. All control stations must respond, plus all other stations available at that time.
